The Light of My Life

Erin Mandapat



She walks into the room,
And everything just shines,
Her smile bright as the sun,
Her love, a pure design.
She fills my life with laughter,
And banishes all my fears,
With her by my side,
I have nothing to fear.
Her eyes, like pools of light,
Reflect the love within,
And every time I see her,
I fall in love again.
Her gentle touch and kind words,
Can soothe even the pain,
And I thank the stars above,
For giving her to me, time and again.
So here's to you, my love,
My heart, my life, my sun,
I thank the heavens above,
For giving me you, my one.
